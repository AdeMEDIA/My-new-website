About us

Nexton Labs is dedicated to develop and market next generation IT products and to carry out technical innovation for the trade, industry and public at large. Nexton labs is based in Lagos, and brings together the craft of software, education and service design. Founded in 2016, Nexton's products reach more than 2 Million learners worldwide.
